About UniVaP

UniVaP (Unified Vaccination Platform) is a product which is developed for both Healthcare workers and patients. To patients it not only keeps track of your vaccinations but also reminds you of when it is time to book your next vaccination appointment. Patients can view the vaccination details and they are authorized to change their account details. It would be an easily accessible product which stores your information in a secure place where you are always able to find it when needed. Healthcare workers can update the vaccination details of an individual and this handy application enables them to access patients' vaccination details for statistical analysis.
